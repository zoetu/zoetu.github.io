Biography

I am a Ph.D student at School of Data Science, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, under the supervision of Prof. Zhizheng Wu. I was the algorithm engineer at Meituan in 2021, focus on speech synthesis. Before that, I was the algorithm engineer at Lenevo Research in 2020. I obtained the master degree in School of Integrated Circuits, Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Shouyi Yin. Previously, I received the BEng degree in Electronic and Information Engineering from Beihang University, Beijing, in 2017.

My research interests include speech synthesis, automatic speech recognition , keyword spotting, etc.
